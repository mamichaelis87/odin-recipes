Linguine with Clam Sauce

Description

A comforting pasta right from my mom's kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of linguine
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp parsley flaks
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 8oz can of clams in water
  • 1 cup of shredded parmasean cheese

Steps

  1. Begin cooking pasta (do this on the side while working through recipe)
  2. In a medium saucepan, add olive and begin to saute garlic. When fragrant (before garlic is brown), add clams with juice and 1/2 the parsley
  3. Bring sauce to a light boil, then lower heat and let it simmer for 10 min
  4. Pour sauce over pasta, and top with parmesean
