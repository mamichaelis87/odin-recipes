Fried Plantains

Description

The best snack you'll ever eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 Plantain, starting to brown
  • oil of choice, I prefer avacado oil but any kind will work!
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a skillet with 1/2 in of oil, and begin to heat on medium high
  2. Chop off ends and peel plantains. Then slice diagonally into 3/4 inch slices
  3. When the oil is hot, place the plantains in. They should bubble immediately, if not, take them out and let the oil get hotter.
  4. Afer a few minutes, flip the plantains. Fry until both sides are beginning to turn golden brown, then remove from the oil
  5. Take a small pot and smash the plantain slices until they are flat but not too thin
  6. Place the smashed slices back in the oil and fry until a dark brown, and remove
  7. Add salt immediately, then let cool before enjoying!
