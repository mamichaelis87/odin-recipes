Fish Tacos

Description

Fish Tacos is one of my favorite meals. Whether you're looking for a refreshing summertime dinner to enjoy on your porch, or a warm comforting wintertime lunch on a Sunday afternoon, this fish taco recipe will satisfy your stomach and your tongue!

This meal is so easy to make your own. Want some spice? Add some cayenne to the fish or pour on your favorite hot sauce. Want to make it diet friendly? Use jicama tortillas to wrap it up. No matter how you like your food, Fish Tacos can be adapted for your taste.

Ingredients

    2 tilapia filets juice of 2 limes salt and pepper 6 corn tortillas 1 cup shredded red cabbage 2 tbsp grapefruit vinegrette 3 tbsp sour cream

Steps

    Step 1: cook tilapia in a skillet with salt, pepper, and juice from 1/2 lime Step 2: soak cabbage in vinegrette Step 3: toast tortillas Step 4: mix remaining lime juice with sour cream Step 5: load up your taco and enjoy!
